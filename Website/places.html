places within the game

Mornington Sports Centre

The Mornington Sports Centre is the place of the squat that tracerats have joined. The anarchist group that squats it has made it into a community project. It looks very chaotic, but cozy!




Saint Paul's Cathedral

Saint Paul's Cathedral is the place where the protest in the beginning of the game takes place. it is one of the places that the group could pick up the player character.




hackerspace

the hackerspace is an area within the squat, that tracerats decided to do their hacking in. it's got cool graffiti and fun little trinkets and colourful blinking LED lights everywhere!




east end

the east end has lots of cool graffitis. mayhem can take the player and storm graffitiing there at some point during the story.




Down Street Station

Down Street Station is an abandoned tube station which was used as an underground bunker during WWII. it is also where dev, nova and the player go for some urban exploring.
